The Point of You Team

A husband-and-wife photography duo

We first met in high school band and shared a love for percussion. We've been creating memories together for over a decade now and have fallen in love with the art of capturing life through the lens. We cover both the main events and the behind-the-scenes moments. One of us tends to focus on the wide shots and the light, the other on the small moments in between. Together, we cover more ground and miss less.

Our style is documentary and relaxed. We'll guide you when it helps, but mostly we're watching for the real stuff. The laugh before the pose, the look between you and the people you love, or when the light hits the trees just right. We catch the really real moments that matter most. Sometimes our best work is accidental and that's what keeps it authentic and exciting for us.
