what we do
— how we work —

Two photographers, one shared eye for the moment

Before any camera comes out, we talk through what your day looks like, who matters most in the frame, and how much coverage you need. We'll then look over the area and plan our approach, looking for the best angles and lighting. As a team, we can split up to catch it all. While one focuses on the main events, the other can capture the details and candid moments that make your day unique. This level of attention to detail doesn't stop at the large events. Even on our smaller packages, we will bring the same level of care that you would expect from an all day event with two photographers. You can be confident that we will give the same high-quality service regardless of the package you choose!
